About

Featuring a fitness centre, bar, garden and free WiFi, Sol Oasis A fitness centre, bar, garden and free wifi are a few of the many perks you will get to enjoy while staying in Sol Oasis Marrakech. Situated a short drive away from the Yuves Saint Laurent Museum, Boucharouite museum, the oriental muesum of Marrakech and Majorelle Gardens accessible via the free service shuttle bus into the city centre. The hotel also offers a 24-hour help desk, kids club, evening entertainment, a year-round outdoor pool and a restaurant which serves a buffet breakfast lunch and dinner. Guests can also enjoy a spa with an indoor pool, 2 tennis courts equipment included, a football field, beach volleyball and basketball. The closest airport which is 13km away is the Marrakech-Menara Airport. All of our rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a kettle, shower and a full set of toiletries
